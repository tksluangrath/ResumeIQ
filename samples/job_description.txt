Senior Python Backend Engineer
Acme Technologies — Remote (US)

--- About the Role ---

We are looking for a Senior Python Backend Engineer to join our platform team and help us scale our
core services from 10 million to 100 million users. You will design and own backend systems that
power real-time data pipelines, developer-facing REST APIs, and internal tooling used by over 200
engineers.

This is a high-impact role for an engineer who cares deeply about correctness, observability, and
system reliability. You will collaborate directly with product, data, and infrastructure teams, and
your work will ship to production within your first two weeks.

--- About the Team ---

The platform engineering team owns the foundational services that all other teams depend on: API
gateways, authentication, data ingestion pipelines, and our internal developer platform. We operate
with a strong bias toward automation, thorough code review, and on-call discipline. We have a small
team of six engineers, and every member ships independently.

--- What You Will Do ---

- Design, build, and maintain high-throughput Python backend services using FastAPI
- Own the full lifecycle of multiple microservices: architecture, implementation, testing, deployment
- Write and maintain PostgreSQL schemas, migrations, and query optimizations
- Build and extend REST APIs consumed by web, mobile, and third-party integrations
- Containerize services with Docker and manage deployments on AWS (ECS, Lambda, S3, RDS)
- Collaborate with the DevOps team to improve CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure-as-code
- Participate in on-call rotation and drive post-mortems for incidents
- Mentor junior engineers through code review and pair programming sessions
- Write clear technical documentation and ADRs (Architecture Decision Records)

--- Requirements ---

- 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
- 3+ years of production Python experience (Python 3.10+)
- Strong experience building and maintaining REST APIs at scale
- Proficiency with FastAPI or equivalent async Python framework (Django, Flask, Litestar)
- Deep knowledge of PostgreSQL: schema design, query tuning, indexing strategies
- Experience with Docker for local development and production deployments
- Hands-on AWS experience: EC2, ECS, RDS, S3, CloudWatch, and IAM
- Solid understanding of Git workflows (trunk-based development, PR reviews, semantic versioning)
- Demonstrated experience writing unit, integration, and end-to-end tests (pytest preferred)
- Strong grasp of software engineering fundamentals: SOLID principles, clean architecture, DRY

--- Nice to Have ---

- Experience with Kubernetes (EKS) or container orchestration at scale
- Familiarity with message queues: Kafka, SQS, or RabbitMQ
- Exposure to Terraform or other infrastructure-as-code tools
- Experience with Redis for caching or distributed locking
- Background in data engineering or experience with tools like Airflow, Spark, or dbt
- GraphQL API design experience
- Open-source contributions or public GitHub portfolio

--- What We Offer ---

- Competitive base salary: $160,000–$190,000 depending on experience
- Equity: 0.05%–0.15% options (4-year vest, 1-year cliff)
- 100% remote, async-first culture with optional team offsites twice per year
- $3,000 annual learning and development budget
- Health, dental, and vision insurance (100% covered for employee)
- Unlimited PTO with a 15-day minimum
- Top-of-the-line MacBook Pro or Linux workstation of your choice

--- Interview Process ---

1. 30-minute recruiter screen
2. 60-minute technical phone screen with a senior engineer (system design + Python fundamentals)
3. Take-home project (4–6 hours): build a small FastAPI service with PostgreSQL and Docker
4. 3-hour virtual onsite: system design, code review exercise, and culture fit
5. Offer within 48 hours of final interview
